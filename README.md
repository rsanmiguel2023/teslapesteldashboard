# Tesla and the Electric Vehicle Revolution: PESTEL Dashboard Analysis

End-to-end data analytics case study analyzing Tesla’s product development and market performance using the PESTEL framework, supported by interactive dashboards and data-driven insights.

---

## Project Overview

This project evaluates Tesla’s position in the electric vehicle (EV) industry by analyzing the impact of Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal (PESTEL) factors.

The analysis focuses on how macroeconomic trends, regulatory policies, technological advancements, and consumer behavior influence Tesla’s growth, risks, and strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Assess external factors affecting Tesla’s performance
* Identify operational risks and opportunities
* Support strategic decision-making using data insights
* Evaluate Tesla’s sustainability and innovation strategy

---

## Methodology

* PESTEL Framework Analysis
* Data Visualization using Power BI dashboards
* Trend and comparative analysis
* Business-focused interpretation of data insights

---

## Key Analysis Areas

### Political Factors

* Government subsidies and EV incentives
* Trade policies and geopolitical impact
* Regulatory environment and lobbying

### Economic Factors

* GDP trends and Tesla growth
* Workforce expansion
* Market share and revenue performance

### Social Factors

* EV adoption trends
* Consumer preferences and segmentation
* Luxury and brand positioning

### Technological Factors

* Battery efficiency and innovation
* Performance vs range trade-offs
* R&D investment impact

### Environmental Factors

* CO₂ emissions reduction
* Battery recycling and sustainability

### Legal Factors

* Regulatory investigations
* Cybersecurity and compliance

## Key Insights
